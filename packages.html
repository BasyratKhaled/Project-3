FAQ

Questions teams usually ask about pricing.

Pricing pages work better when they reduce doubts with short, honest answers instead of hiding details.

Can we start small instead of a full project?

Yes. Many teams start with the Starter audit to understand their current product and security state before committing to a Core or Enterprise engagement.

Are these prices fixed or ranges?

They are starting points. After we understand your scope, timelines, and integrations, we lock a clear price inside that tier—no surprise add‑ons.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We work remotely with teams from different countries and align on time zones, communication tools, and security requirements.

What if I only need security help?

We can do security‑only audits, hardening, or monitoring and incident support as a focused engagement or add‑on to existing products.
